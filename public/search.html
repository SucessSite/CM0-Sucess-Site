Software Engineering Intern - Apple

Description The team is looking for a self-motivated individual with excellent verbal & written communication skills who is willing to question assumptions, take end to end ownership of features and drive them to completion. You should have experience developing large-scale server-side systems using object-oriented languages, working on complex problems and distributed systems where scalability and performance are very important. You will work independently within a small team of experts, while also collaborating effectively with members of this and other teams throughout software development lifecycle. Ability to pick up new technologies quickly is also a key quality we seek. Our customers love what we do at Apple and we want the same from our engineers, so bring passion for building the best systems! We love figuring out how things work and trying to make them better. Sometimes it's a little tweak, sometimes it's a fundamental shift - usually it is somewhere in between. We brainstorm on whiteboards, share ideas, build prototypes, present proposals to teams, embed for a while to help them get started, check the metrics, and repeat!
